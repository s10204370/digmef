The Red Room

Have you ever had a lucid dream? Those dreams are just our minds dealing with the day's events, right? There's nothing paranormal about that; everyone has a lucid dream at least once in their lives. I mean, there are places you visit often in your dreams. A certain house, a shop or a school for example... And these dreams are just a figments of our imaginations, right?

BUT WHY DO I ALWAYS DREAM ABOUT CREEPY ACTIVITIES IN MY ROOM?!

I don't know if I'm going mad or something but I keep having a lucid dream that places me in my room! It all started when I visited a website called the Red Room on the deep web. I thought it was all fun and jest when it said it will open my third eye but I started seeing some weird creepy things in my dreams! The whole room was red and black and there's ghost or demons or whatever you call them!

I tried to take a picture in one of my dreams to prove to myself that it was all just a dream.
BUT WHY DO I STILL SEE THAT PICTURE IN MY GALLERY WHEN I WAKE UP! I KEEP TELLING MY FAMILY AND FRIENDS ABOUT IT BUT THEY THINK I'M GOING INSANE! I'M GOING TO DOCUMENT MY DREAM AND PROVE TO EVERYONE THAT I'M NOT GOING CRAZY!

On the second dream I'm documenting, I found this note.. Someone is watching me and I have to get out of here.

The note said that I need to open the door to leave this room but the door is locked.. I guess I need to find a key to open the door in order to get out.

This is the third dream I'm documenting and things seems to be becoming worse..

I saw a girl in my room. She seems to be telling me something but I couldn't understand because her lips were stitched together. I've been checking closets and cabinets for the key but I can't seem to find it.

This is the fourth time I'm recording my dreams. She has a knife now and she's cutting the stitches on her lips and slicing off her eyelids!

HELP! She was going to kill me but I managed to wake up! I think she is going to kill me in my dreams tonight!

If anyone is seeing this, investigate the Red Room and help me!
